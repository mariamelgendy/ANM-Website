A Legacy of Excellence in Garment Manufacturing

For over two decades, ANM Garments has been at the forefront of the global textile and garment manufacturing industry. Headquartered in the Alexandria Free Zone, we are dedicated to crafting high-quality apparel for prestigious global brands, offering designs that blend creativity with functionality.

Our in-house brand, KleanCorp, is a testament to our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and inclusivity. By combining modern technology with skilled craftsmanship, we deliver garments that redefine quality and sophistication.

  • Precision and Innovation

    Our advanced facilities and cutting-edge technology ensure flawless production processes and exceptional garment quality.

  • Global Presence

    We proudly serve international markets with offices in Cairo and partnerships across Europe, North America, and the Middle East.

Our Vision

To be a global benchmark for excellence in garment manufacturing, committed to innovation, sustainability, and social responsibility.
